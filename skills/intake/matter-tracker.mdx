## Vấn đề giải quyết

LS firm có 50+ active matter — cần dashboard nhanh trạng thái + milestone + giờ billable per case.

## Output

- `matter_status.json` — open / in-progress / on-hold / closed
- Timeline: intake date → key milestones → close date
- Billable hours aggregate per lawyer

## Hỗ trợ workflow

- Partner partner review caseload weekly
- Billing department invoice prep
- Client status reporting
